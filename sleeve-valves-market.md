<p><p>The sleeve valves market comprises three main types based on design and application. The Straight Inline Type features a linear flow path, ensuring minimal pressure loss, ideal for straightforward flow applications. The Submerged Discharge Type is designed for environments where the valve is submerged, providing reliable control in tanks or underwater systems. The Angle Inline Type, with its angular design, facilitates efficient flow redirection, making it suitable for space-constrained installations. Each type caters to specific operational requirements and environments.</p></p>
