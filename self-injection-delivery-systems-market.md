<p><strong>The Self-Injection Delivery Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intramuscular</li><li>Intravenous</li><li>Sub-Cutaneous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Self-injection delivery systems are categorized into three main types: intramuscular, intravenous, and subcutaneous. Intramuscular systems deliver medication deep into muscle tissue, providing rapid absorption for vaccines and hormones. Intravenous systems administer drugs directly into the bloodstream, ensuring immediate systemic effects, often used in emergency care. Subcutaneous systems are designed for injecting medication into the fatty tissue beneath the skin, commonly used for insulin and biologics, allowing for slower absorption and extended effects. Each type serves specific therapeutic needs and patient preferences.</p></p>
