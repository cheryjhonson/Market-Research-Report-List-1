<p><ul><li>Vulcanizer with Rotor</li><li>Rotorless Vulcanizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vulcameter market primarily includes two types of vulcanizers: the vulcanizer with rotor and the rotorless vulcanizer. The vulcanizer with rotor features a rotating mechanism that ensures uniform heat and pressure distribution, making it ideal for processing rubber samples. In contrast, the rotorless vulcanizer utilizes a fixed chamber design, which allows for more consistent sample preparation without mechanical stirring. Both types are essential in rubber manufacturing, enabling accurate measurement of vulcanization properties for quality control.</p></p>
