<p><p>The aeroplane carbon brake disc market is categorized into three main types: single disc brakes, dual disc brakes, and multiple disc brakes. Single disc brakes feature one disc per wheel and are often used in smaller aircraft for weight efficiency. Dual disc brakes involve two discs per wheel, providing enhanced stopping power and heat dissipation, suitable for larger commercial jets. Multiple disc brakes consist of several discs stacked together, delivering superior braking performance and thermal management, ideal for high-performance planes and heavy cargo aircraft.</p></p>
