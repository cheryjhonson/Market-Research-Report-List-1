<p><p>The digital inkjet printing on textiles market encompasses several key types. Direct-to-garment printing involves applying ink directly onto finished garments, allowing for intricate designs and short runs. Direct-to-fabric printing applies ink onto fabric before it's cut, ideal for larger production volumes. Dye sublimation printing transfers dye onto synthetic fabrics using heat, resulting in vibrant colors and durability. Care label printing focuses on producing labels with necessary care instructions, often using specialized inks to ensure clarity and longevity in various conditions.</p></p>
