<p><p>The Collation Shrink Film market includes Monolayer and Multilayer types. Monolayer Collation Shrink Film is made from a single layer of polymer, offering cost-effectiveness and basic protection for bundling products like bottles and cans. Multilayer Collation Shrink Film consists of multiple polymer layers, providing enhanced durability, puncture resistance, and better load stability, ideal for heavy or irregularly shaped items. Both types are widely used in packaging industries to secure and protect grouped products during transportation and storage.</p></p>
