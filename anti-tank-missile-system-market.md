<p><p>The Anti-Tank Missile System market is primarily categorized into two types: Man-Portable Anti-Tank Missiles (MPATMs) and Vehicle-Mounted Anti-Tank Missiles. MPATMs are lightweight, portable systems designed for individual soldiers, providing flexibility and quick deployment against armored threats. In contrast, Vehicle-Mounted Anti-Tank Missiles are integrated into military vehicles, enhancing firepower and range while allowing for stable targeting and rapid engagement of enemy tanks. Both types play crucial roles in modern warfare, adapting to diverse operational needs and environments.</p></p>
