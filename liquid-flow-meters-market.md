<p><strong>The Liquid Flow Meters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Displacement Flow Meters</li><li>Turbine Flow Meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid flow meters market includes various types such as positive displacement flow meters and turbine flow meters. Positive displacement flow meters measure the volume of liquid by trapping fixed amounts and counting the cycles, providing high accuracy for low flow rates. Turbine flow meters, on the other hand, utilize a spinning turbine to gauge flow rate based on the velocity of the liquid passing through. Both types are essential in industries like oil and gas, water treatment, and chemical processing for precise flow measurement.</p></p>
