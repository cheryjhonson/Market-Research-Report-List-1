<p><p>The medical compression socks market is segmented into two primary types: knee-high and thigh-high socks. Thigh-high compression socks provide greater coverage and support, making them suitable for more severe conditions like chronic venous disorders and deep vein thrombosis. Both types are designed to improve circulation, reduce swelling, and provide relief from discomfort associated with prolonged sitting or standing.</p></p>
