<p><p>The Automotive Constant Velocity (CV) Joint market includes Outboard Joints and Inboard Joints, both critical for smooth power transmission in vehicles. Outboard Joints connect the wheel hub to the driveshaft, handling steering and suspension movements. Inboard Joints link the driveshaft to the transmission, accommodating axial movement and vibrations. Both types ensure efficient torque transfer while maintaining flexibility, enhancing vehicle performance and durability. The market for these joints is driven by demand for reliable, high-performance components in passenger and commercial vehicles.</p></p>
