<p><p>The eco-friendly cat litter market consists of two main types: non-clumping and clumping. Non-clumping litter absorbs moisture without forming solid clumps, typically made from materials like recycled paper, corn, or wheat, making it biodegradable and safe for the environment. Conversely, clumping litter forms solid masses upon contact with moisture, usually made from natural clay or plant-based products. Both types cater to environmentally-conscious pet owners seeking sustainable alternatives that minimize their ecological footprint while maintaining effective odor control and cleanliness.</p></p>
