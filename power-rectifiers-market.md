<p><p>The power rectifiers market primarily consists of diode rectifiers and thyristor rectifiers. Diode rectifiers are used for converting alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC) with simplicity and efficiency, making them ideal for basic applications. Thyristor rectifiers, on the other hand, are more advanced, allowing control over power flow and enabling applications in high-voltage and high-current scenarios. Their ability to handle larger loads and operate at higher frequencies makes them suitable for industrial applications and power management systems.</p></p>
