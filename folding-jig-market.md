Semi-automatic jigs require some manual input while offering enhanced efficiency compared to manual systems. Manual folding jigs are entirely operated by hand, providing greater flexibility and lower costs, suited for small-scale or specialized tasks. Each type caters to different production needs and operational scales.</p></p>
