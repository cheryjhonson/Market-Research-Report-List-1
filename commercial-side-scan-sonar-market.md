<p><p>The commercial side scan sonar market is primarily categorized into single-beam and multi-beam systems. Single-beam sonar uses one sonar beam to create a detailed image of the seafloor, making it suitable for focused surveys. In contrast, multi-beam sonar employs multiple beams simultaneously, covering a wider area and providing more detailed and comprehensive seafloor maps. Both technologies are crucial for marine exploration, underwater archaeology, and environmental monitoring, with each type offering distinct advantages depending on the application and survey requirements.</p></p>
