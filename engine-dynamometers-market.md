<p><p>Engine dynamometers measure engine performance by assessing power output and efficiency. Eddy current dynamometers utilize electromagnetic fields to create resistance, allowing for controlled testing without mechanical wear. AC dynamometers use alternating current to generate load, suitable for various applications. Hydraulic dynamometers employ fluid dynamics to absorb engine power, providing precise measurements. High-speed dynamometers are designed for testing engines running at high RPMs, ensuring stability and accuracy. Other types include various specialized dynamometers catering to unique engine testing needs.</p></p>
