<p><p>Engineering liability insurance encompasses various market types, notably Directors and Officers (D&O) insurance and Errors and Omissions (E&O) insurance. D&O insurance protects company executives from personal losses due to legal actions arising from their managerial decisions, safeguarding them against claims of wrongful acts. E&O insurance, on the other hand, covers professionals against claims of inadequate work or negligence in providing services, ensuring financial stability when clients seek compensation for perceived errors or omissions. Together, they mitigate risks in the engineering sector.</p></p>
