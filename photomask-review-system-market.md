<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Photomask Review System market is divided into two primary types: Die to Die (DD) and Die to Database (DB) methods. The DD method focuses on comparing individual die images from different photomasks to ensure pattern accuracy and quality, facilitating defect identification. In contrast, the DB method utilizes a comprehensive database of previously verified dies to assess new photomasks, streamlining the review process. Both methods are essential for maintaining high standards in semiconductor manufacturing and enhancing production efficiency.</p></p>
