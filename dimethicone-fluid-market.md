<p><p>The Dimethicone Fluid market is categorized into three primary types based on viscosity: low, medium, and high. Low viscosity dimethicone is often used for lightweight applications, providing a silky feel and enhanced spreadability in cosmetic formulations. Medium viscosity dimethicone strikes a balance, offering both smoothing properties and moisture retention, ideal for lotions and creams. High viscosity dimethicone delivers greater thickness and stability, making it suitable for products requiring increased film-forming capabilities or enhanced durability, such as hair waxes and heavy creams.</p></p>
