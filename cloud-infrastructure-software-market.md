<p><p>The cloud infrastructure software market encompasses three main types: Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS), Platform as a Service (PaaS), and Software as a Service (SaaS). IaaS provides virtualized computing resources over the internet, enabling users to manage infrastructure without physical hardware. PaaS offers development platforms for building and deploying applications, facilitating collaboration without managing underlying infrastructure. SaaS delivers software applications through the cloud, allowing users to access and use them via the internet, eliminating local installations and maintenance.</p></p>
