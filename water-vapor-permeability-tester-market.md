<p><strong>The Water Vapor Permeability Tester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravimetric Method (Cup Method)</li><li>Electrolytic Detection Sensor Method</li><li>Infrared Detection Sensor Method</li><li>Humidity Detection Sensor Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Water Vapor Permeability Tester market comprises several methods for measuring moisture transmission through materials. The Gravimetric Method (Cup Method) weighs samples before and after exposure to humidity changes to calculate permeability. The Electrolytic Detection Sensor Method uses sensors to detect water vapor levels electrically. The Infrared Detection Sensor Method measures vapor absorption based on infrared light interaction, while the Humidity Detection Sensor Method focuses on changes in humidity levels, providing reliable assessments of material permeability for various applications.</p></p>
