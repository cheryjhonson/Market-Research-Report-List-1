<p><p>The dental biomaterial market is segmented into two main types: natural dental products and biocompatible synthetic dental products. Natural dental products are derived from biological sources and offer benefits such as reduced toxicity and enhanced integration with biological tissues. In contrast, biocompatible synthetic dental products are engineered materials designed to mimic natural properties while providing favorable mechanical and chemical characteristics. Both types aim to improve dental health outcomes, longevity of treatments, and patient comfort in restorative and prosthetic dentistry.</p></p>
