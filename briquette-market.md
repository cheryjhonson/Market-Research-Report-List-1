<p><p>The briquette market includes various types, each with distinct shapes and production methods. **Solid Cylindrical Type** briquettes are dense, uniform cylinders, ideal for consistent burning in industrial and domestic settings. **RUF Type** briquettes are rectangular, compact blocks, known for high density and durability, often used in heating systems. **Pini-Kay Type** briquettes are cylindrical with a central hole, offering efficient combustion and high energy output, commonly used in stoves and boilers. Each type caters to specific applications based on shape, density, and combustion efficiency.</p></p>
