<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle Airbag Inflator market is categorized into three main types: pyrotechnic inflators, stored gas inflators, and hybrid inflators. Pyrotechnic inflators utilize combustion reactions to generate gas rapidly for airbag deployment. Stored gas inflators use compressed gas stored in a canister for inflating airbags. Hybrid inflators combine both technologies, offering advantages like faster deployment and adjustable inflation pressures. These advancements aim to enhance passenger safety in electric and hybrid vehicles, adapting to the unique requirements of new energy platforms.</p></p>
