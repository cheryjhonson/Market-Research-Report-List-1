<p><p>The ampoule filling machine market is segmented into open type and closed type systems. Open type machines are designed for environments where exposure to air is permissible, allowing for easy access and quick setup; they are typically less expensive but may require additional sterilization processes. Closed type machines operate in a controlled environment, minimizing contamination risks and ensuring product integrity. They are often used for sensitive pharmaceuticals, providing a more secure filling process but at a higher cost.</p></p>
