<p><p>Healthcare Enterprise Content Management (ECM) involves the systematic management of healthcare information and documents to enhance operational efficiency and compliance. The market is divided into two main types: hosted and on-premises. Hosted ECM solutions are cloud-based, allowing for scalability, remote access, and lower upfront costs. In contrast, on-premises ECM solutions are installed locally within the healthcare facility, providing greater control over data security and customization but often requiring higher maintenance and IT resources. Both types aim to improve healthcare document management and patient care.</p></p>
