<p><strong>The Carbon Steel Flanges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blind Flange</li><li>Weld Neck Flange</li><li>Slip-On Flange</li><li>Socket Weld Flange</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon steel flanges market comprises various types, each serving specific applications. Blind flanges are used to seal ends of pipelines, providing no opening. Weld neck flanges are designed for high-pressure applications, allowing for better alignment during welding. Slip-on flanges easily slide over the pipe and are welded for a secure fit. Socket weld flanges enable pipes to fit into the flange, ideal for high-pressure systems. Other types may include threaded and lap joint flanges, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
