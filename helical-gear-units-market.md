<p><p>The helical gear units market is primarily divided into two types: parallel-axis and perpendicular-axis helical gear units. Parallel-axis helical gear units feature shafts aligned parallel to each other, providing efficient power transmission and smooth operation, ideal for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, perpendicular-axis helical gear units have shafts positioned at a 90-degree angle, allowing for compact designs and versatility in space-constrained environments. Both types are widely used across various industries, including automotive, manufacturing, and aerospace, due to their reliability and performance.</p></p>
