<p><ul><li>Self-inflating Resuscitator</li><li>Flow-inflating Resuscitator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiac resuscitation devices market comprises various types, primarily including self-inflating and flow-inflating resuscitators. Self-inflating resuscitators are compact devices that function autonomously, allowing for manual ventilation without the need for an external gas source. In contrast, flow-inflating resuscitators rely on a continuous gas supply, requiring a connected oxygen source to provide ventilation. Both types are essential in emergency medical situations, offering healthcare professionals vital tools for restoring adequate breathing and circulation in patients undergoing cardiac arrest.</p></p>
