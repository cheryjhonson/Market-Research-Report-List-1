<p><p>The Scanning Probe Microscopes (SPM) market encompasses various types including Atomic Force Microscopes (AFM), Scanning Tunneling Microscopes (STM), and Near-field Scanning Optical Microscopes (NSOM). AFMs measure surface topography by using a fine tip to scan over samples, providing high-resolution images of nanostructures. STMs exploit quantum tunneling to visualize conductive surfaces at the atomic level. NSOM combines optical microscopy with scanning probe techniques, enabling imaging below the diffraction limit by utilizing near-field light interactions, thus broadening applications in materials science and biology.</p></p>
