<p><p>The Vertical Cavity Surface Emitter Laser (VCSEL) market is primarily divided into two types: Single Mode VCSEL and Multimode VCSEL. Single Mode VCSELs emit light through a single spatial mode, providing high precision and low divergence, making them ideal for applications like telecommunications and data centers. In contrast, Multimode VCSELs emit light through multiple modes, allowing for a higher output power and broader bandwidth, which is useful in applications such as short-distance communication and sensor technology.</p></p>
