<p><p>The Glass Bulb Sprinklers market includes various types designed for different applications. Pendent sprinklers are installed on the ceiling and distribute water downward, ideal for open spaces. Upright sprinklers are mounted upright on pipes, releasing water in a 360-degree pattern, suitable for warehouses. Horizontal sidewall sprinklers are designed to protect walls and are mounted on the sides, ideal for narrow spaces. Concealed sprinklers hide within the ceiling, offering a discreet appearance while effectively suppressing fire.</p></p>
