<p><strong>The Cylinder Liner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Cylinder Liner</li><li>Wet Cylinder Liner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cylinder liner market comprises two main types: dry cylinder liners and wet cylinder liners. Dry cylinder liners are installed with a clearance from the engine block, relying on oil for lubrication, making them lightweight and suitable for high-performance engines. In contrast, wet cylinder liners are integrated with the engine block, allowing coolant to circulate around them, which aids in temperature regulation. Both types serve crucial roles in enhancing engine performance and durability, catering to various automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
