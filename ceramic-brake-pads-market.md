<p><strong>The Ceramic Brake Pads Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Copper Free Ceramic Brake Pads</li><li>Copper-bearing Ceramic Brake Pad</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ceramic brake pads market includes **copper-free** and **copper-bearing** types. Copper-free ceramic brake pads are eco-friendly, designed to reduce environmental impact by eliminating copper, which can harm aquatic ecosystems. They offer quiet, low-dust performance with excellent heat resistance. Copper-bearing ceramic brake pads contain copper for enhanced thermal conductivity and braking efficiency, ideal for high-performance vehicles. Both types provide durability and reduced wear, but copper-free variants align with stricter environmental regulations, while copper-bearing pads cater to demanding driving conditions.</p></p>
