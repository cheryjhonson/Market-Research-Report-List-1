<p><p>The automotive semi-solid state LiDAR market includes two key types: micro vibrating mirrors and rotating mirrors. They are ideal for applications requiring lightweight and space-efficient solutions. In contrast, rotating mirrors employ a pivoting mechanism to scan environments, providing broader coverage and effective range. Both technologies cater to advanced driver assistance systems and autonomous vehicles, enhancing safety and navigation capabilities in diverse conditions.</p></p>
