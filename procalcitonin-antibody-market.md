<p><p>The Procalcitonin Antibody market is primarily divided into two types: polyclonal and monoclonal antibodies. Polyclonal Procalcitonin Antibodies are generated from multiple B-cell lineages, providing a mixture of different antibodies that recognize various epitopes on the procalcitonin protein. In contrast, Monoclonal Procalcitonin Antibodies are produced from a single B-cell clone, offering a uniform and highly specific response to a specific epitope. Both types are crucial for diagnosing and monitoring infections and inflammation, particularly in critical care settings.</p></p>
