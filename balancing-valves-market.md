In contrast, automatic balancing valves use built-in mechanisms to adjust flow dynamically, ensuring optimal performance without manual intervention. This innovation enhances system efficiency and reduces energy consumption. Both types are essential for maintaining system balance and improving operational efficiency in various applications, such as HVAC and industrial processes.</p></p>
