<p><p>The roll briquetting machine market is segmented into three main types based on transmission methods: mechanical, hydraulic, and pneumatic. Mechanical transmission uses gears and belts for efficient power transfer and is praised for its reliability. Hydraulic transmission employs hydraulic pressure for compacting materials, offering superior density and flexibility in production. Pneumatic transmission utilizes air pressure for material handling and shaping, promoting faster operation and reduced wear on components. Each type serves diverse industrial applications in briquette production.</p></p>
