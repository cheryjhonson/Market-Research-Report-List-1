The fuel tank stores fuel and must be vapor-tight. Gas caps prevent vapor escape. Liquid vapor separators manage liquid fuel and vapor transition. Control valves regulate the flow of vapors, and sensors detect vapor levels and leaks. Hoses connect these components, ensuring efficient vapor management. Together, these elements help comply with environmental regulations while enhancing vehicle performance.</p></p>
