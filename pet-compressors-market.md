<p><strong>The PET Compressors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reciprocating Type</li><li>Screw Type</li><li>Centrifugal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PET compressors market comprises three main types: reciprocating, screw, and centrifugal compressors. Reciprocating compressors utilize pistons to compress gas, making them suitable for high-pressure applications with a compact design. Screw compressors use two interlocking helical screws, providing continuous flow and efficiency, ideal for larger operations. Centrifugal compressors leverage high-speed rotation to achieve compression, offering high capacity and efficiency for medium to large-scale processes. Each type serves distinct industrial needs within the PET production and processing sectors.</p></p>
