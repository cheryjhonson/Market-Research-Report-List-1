<p><p>The detox fiber drink market comprises two main types: soluble fiber drinks and insoluble fiber drinks. Soluble fiber drinks dissolve in water, forming a gel-like substance that aids in digestion, helps regulate blood sugar levels, and promotes a feeling of fullness. In contrast, insoluble fiber drinks do not dissolve in water and provide bulk to the digestive system, facilitating regular bowel movements and preventing constipation. Both types cater to health-conscious consumers seeking wellness benefits through fiber supplementation.</p></p>
