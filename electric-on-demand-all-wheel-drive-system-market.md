<p><p>The Electric On-Demand All-Wheel Drive (AWD) System market includes three main types: Battery Drive System, Hybrid Drive System, and Plug-in Hybrid Drive System. The **Battery Drive System** relies solely on electric power, offering instant torque and zero emissions. The **Hybrid Drive System** combines an internal combustion engine with an electric motor, optimizing fuel efficiency and performance. The **Plug-in Hybrid Drive System** allows external charging, offering extended electric-only range while retaining the flexibility of a hybrid for longer trips. All systems enhance traction and control through on-demand AWD capabilities.</p></p>
