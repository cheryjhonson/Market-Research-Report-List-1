<p><p>The alkalinity control chemicals market encompasses substances used to manage pH levels in various applications, particularly in water treatment. Two primary types are acids and bases. Acids, such as sulfuric or hydrochloric acid, are used to lower pH and neutralize alkaline conditions. Conversely, bases, like sodium hydroxide or calcium carbonate, are employed to increase pH and achieve desired alkalinity levels. The choice between these chemicals is influenced by factors like water chemistry, treatment goals, and environmental regulations.</p></p>
