<p><p>Stacker cranes are automated systems used for warehousing and material handling, categorized into two main types: monocolumn and two-column stacker cranes. Monocolumn stacker cranes feature a single vertical mast, offering a compact design ideal for narrow aisle applications, maximizing space efficiency. In contrast, two-column stacker cranes consist of two vertical masts, providing enhanced stability and increased lifting capacity, making them suitable for larger and heavier loads. Both types improve operational efficiency in storage and retrieval processes.</p></p>
