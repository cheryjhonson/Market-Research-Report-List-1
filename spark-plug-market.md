<p><strong>The Spark Plug Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Spark Plugs</li><li>Cold Spark Plugs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **Spark Plug Market** includes **Hot Spark Plugs** and **Cold Spark Plugs**, differentiated by their heat range. **Hot Spark Plugs** have a longer insulator nose, retaining more heat, making them ideal for low-speed, low-compression engines to prevent fouling. **Cold Spark Plugs** have a shorter insulator nose, dissipating heat faster, suitable for high-performance, high-compression engines to avoid overheating and pre-ignition. Both types cater to specific engine requirements, balancing heat retention and dissipation for optimal performance and longevity in various automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
