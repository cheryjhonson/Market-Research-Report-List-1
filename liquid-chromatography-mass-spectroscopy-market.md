<p><p>The Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectroscopy (LC-MS) market includes various types: </p><p>1. **Triple Quadrupole** systems are favored for their sensitivity and precision in quantifying compounds, ideal for trace analysis.  </p><p>2. **Time of Flight (ToF)** instruments excel in measuring molecular weights rapidly, suitable for complex mixtures.  </p><p>3. **Quadrupole-Time of Flight (Q-ToF)** combines high-resolution accurate mass measurements with the flexibility of quadrupole for enhanced analytical capabilities.  </p><p>4. **Others** encompass diverse setups like ion trap and portable systems, catering to specialized applications and diverse analytical needs.</p></p>
