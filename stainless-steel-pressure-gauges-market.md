Lower mount gauges feature connections at the bottom, ideal for equipment with confined spaces. Back mount gauges, with connections at the rear, are designed for panel installations, commonly used in control rooms and machinery. Panel mount gauges integrate seamlessly into control panels, providing a clear readout while conserving space. Each type offers durability and accuracy, making them suitable for a wide range of industrial environments.</p></p>
