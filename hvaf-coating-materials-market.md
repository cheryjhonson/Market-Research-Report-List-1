<p><p>HVAF (High Velocity Air Fuel) coating materials are advanced thermal spray coatings applied using a process that utilizes high-speed air to accelerate fuel combustion gases. Technological advancements and innovations in coating materials are also contributing to market expansion.</p><p>Moreover, the growing emphasis on improving equipment lifespan and reducing maintenance costs is prompting industries to adopt HVAF coatings extensively.
