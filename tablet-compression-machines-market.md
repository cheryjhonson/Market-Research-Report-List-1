<p><p>Tablet compression machines are essential in pharmaceutical manufacturing and are categorized into two main types: single station and multiple station machines. Single station type machines compress one tablet at a time, offering simplicity and ease of operation, ideal for small-scale production or research and development. In contrast, multiple station machines can compress several tablets simultaneously, significantly increasing production efficiency and output, making them suitable for large-scale manufacturing operations where speed and volume are critical.</p></p>
