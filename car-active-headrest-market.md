<p><strong>The Car Active Headrest Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Headrest Activation</li><li>Seat Back Activation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Car Active Headrest market includes two main types: Headrest Activation and Seat Back Activation. Headrest Activation systems use sensors in the headrest to detect rear-end collisions, instantly moving the headrest forward to support the head and reduce whiplash injuries. Seat Back Activation systems integrate sensors in the seat back, triggering the headrest to adjust upon impact. Both systems enhance safety by minimizing neck strain during collisions, with Headrest Activation focusing on the headrest and Seat Back Activation leveraging the seat's structure for responsiveness.</p></p>
