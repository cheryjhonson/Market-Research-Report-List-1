<p><p>The rapeseed protein market comprises various types, including hydrolyzed rapeseed protein and rapeseed protein isolate. Hydrolyzed rapeseed protein is produced through enzymatic or acidic methods, enhancing its solubility and digestibility, making it suitable for sports nutrition and functional foods. Rapeseed protein isolate, on the other hand, is derived through processes that remove fats and carbohydrates, yielding a high-purity protein product ideal for plant-based protein formulations and food additives. Both types cater to the growing demand for sustainable protein sources.</p></p>
