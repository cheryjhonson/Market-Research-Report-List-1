<p><p>The Kitchen Waste System market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. **Monolithic System**: This integrated approach uses a single unit for waste treatment, often combining multiple processes that effectively manage and reduce waste in a compact design.</p><p>2. **Biological System**: Utilizing natural processes, these systems rely on microorganisms to decompose organic waste, often seen in composting setups that convert waste into usable compost.</p><p>3. **Mechanical System**: These systems rely on machines like grinders and shredders to process waste, focusing on physical reduction, often preceding biological treatment or disposal methods.</p></p>
