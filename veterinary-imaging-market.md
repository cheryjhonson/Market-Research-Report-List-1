<p><p>The veterinary imaging market is primarily categorized into X-ray technology, ultrasound technology, and other imaging modalities. X-ray technology provides detailed images of bones and certain organs, aiding in diagnosing fractures, tumors, and infections. Ultrasound technology uses sound waves for real-time imaging of soft tissues and organs, facilitating assessments of pregnancy, heart conditions, and abdominal issues. Other imaging techniques include MRI and CT scans, which offer advanced insights into complex medical conditions, enhancing diagnostic accuracy and treatment planning in veterinary care.</p></p>
