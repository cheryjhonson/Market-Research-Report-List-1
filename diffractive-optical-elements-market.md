<p><strong>The Diffractive Optical Elements Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Beam Shaping (Top-Hat)</li><li>Beam Splitting</li><li>Beam Foci</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Diffractive Optical Elements (DOE) market includes various types tailored for specific optical applications. Beam shaping elements, such as top-hat profiles, create uniform intensity distributions across a surface, ideal for applications in laser machining and biomedicine. Beam splitting elements divide a single beam into multiple outputs, commonly used in spectroscopy and imaging systems. Beam foci elements focus light into specific points, enhancing precision in applications like optical trapping and microscopy. Each type serves unique requirements in advanced optical systems.</p></p>
