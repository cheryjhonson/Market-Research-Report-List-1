<p><p>The vacuum check valves market encompasses several types designed to prevent backflow in various systems. Lift check valves utilize a disc that lifts off its seat, allowing flow in one direction. Swing check valves feature a hinged disc that swings open, facilitating forward flow while preventing reverse flow. Butterfly check valves use a rotating disc to control flow, offering a compact design. Each type serves distinct applications, emphasizing reliability and efficiency in maintaining unidirectional flow within vacuum systems.</p></p>
