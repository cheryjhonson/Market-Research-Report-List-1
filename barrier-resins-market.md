<p><p>The barrier resins market consists of materials designed to impede gas and moisture permeation in packaging applications. Key types include EVOH (ethylene vinyl alcohol), known for its excellent gas barrier properties, making it suitable for food and beverage packaging. PVDC (polyvinylidene chloride) offers high resistance to moisture and oxygen, enhancing shelf life. Other resins in the market may include various blends and emerging materials that enhance barrier performance further.</p></p>
