<p><p>Long-Term Evolution (LTE) for the Internet of Things (IoT) encompasses two primary market types: Narrowband IoT (NB-IoT) and LTE Machine-Type Communications (LTE-M). NB-IoT is designed for low-power, wide-area applications, providing extended coverage and support for a massive number of devices with minimal energy consumption. Conversely, LTE-M supports higher data rates and allows for mobility, making it suitable for applications like wearables and vehicles. Both technologies aim to enhance connectivity for IoT devices, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
