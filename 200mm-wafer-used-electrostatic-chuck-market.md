<p><p>The 200mm wafer electrostatic chuck market primarily includes two types: Coulomb Type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type. The Coulomb Type utilizes electrostatic forces generated by applying voltage to hold the wafer in place, providing stability and precision during processing. In contrast, the JR Type employs a specific design leveraging a combination of electric fields and surface features to enhance the grip on the wafer while minimizing thermal effects. Both types are essential for semiconductor manufacturing, ensuring efficient handling and processing of wafers.</p></p>
